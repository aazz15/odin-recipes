Lasagna

Description

This fresh made lasagna is something that every Italian-food lover should try out!

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Fresh basil
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ground beef
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare the oven to 200 degrees celsius
  2. Mix the ingredients in a oven heatable container
  3. Put the container in the oven for 30 minutes
  4. Enjoy your fresh lasagna!
